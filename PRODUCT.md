# World Checkers Product Context

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
World Checkers is a competitive online draughts arena for International Draughts and Russian checkers. It combines quick play, live games, tournaments, puzzles, lessons, rankings, profiles, and premium analysis in one focused product surface.

## Users
Competitive board-game players who want fast access to play, replay, study, and rankings. The product should support newcomers learning checkers while still feeling credible to rated players who understand time controls, variants, titles, and tournament schedules.

## Design Principles
- The board is the product center. Layouts should support play, analysis, and scanning before promotion.
- Use familiar product patterns: top navigation, dense lists, segmented controls, tables, compact panels, and clear primary actions.
- Keep the arena serious and composed. Avoid decorative spectacle that competes with the board or rating data.
- Make state obvious: selected moves, live games, active clocks, current navigation, chosen filters, and disabled choices need visible affordances.
- Responsive behavior should preserve the task: game setup and rankings collapse cleanly, the board remains square, and controls stay reachable.

## Tone
Calm, precise, competitive, and encouraging. Copy should be short and task-oriented.

## Anti-References
- Marketing-heavy chess templates with giant hero claims and vague stats.
- Neon esports dashboards that overpower the board.
- Over-carded SaaS layouts where every section looks identical.
- Decorative gradients, glass panels, and gimmicky motion.
